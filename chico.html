1. Exploring the Internal Profile of Chico

Chico (Manilkara zapota) is a tropical fruit widely grown in the Philippines. It has a brown, rough exterior with a sweet, malty, and grainy flesh inside. The flavor is often likened to brown sugar or caramel, making it a beloved snack among Filipinos.
